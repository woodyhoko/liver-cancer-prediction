Scanning every gene (schematic volcano)

Each dot is a gene: x = how different the group means are, y = −log₁₀(p). Genes toward the top separate survivors from non-survivors the most — the candidates this exploratory screen surfaces. No multiple-testing correction is applied, so these are leads, not confirmed biomarkers.
